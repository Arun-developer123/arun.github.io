KaamLink — Verified Skilled Marketplace

KaamLink connects verified skilled workers (labours, electricians, plumbers, camera crews, technicians) directly with customers — removing intermediaries and ensuring fair, timely payouts backed by platform protections.

  • Direct access to verified talent — no middleman.
  • Skill endorsements, portfolio checks and on-site verification.
  • Escrow payments & transparent fees.
  • Worker welfare support and guidance to government schemes.
  • Autonomous matching to find the right worker quickly.
